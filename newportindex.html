About Me

'Saving code' === 'Saving lives' "When looking for a career, find something you love to do, then find a way to make a lot of money doing it." @RandomQouteIHeardOnce At this very moment I am a skilled blood banker at a level 1 trauma center. My job makes me proud but like MarieKondo would say, if it doesn't spark joy, let it go. Okay, I'm done inspiring.... Culture and travel are my passion. I found myself building a platform on social networks sharing photos and my experience as a woman of color traveling around the world. I decided to start a website/blog to have my content in one place. I didn't care for the themed layouts online, so I decided to build my own. This is where the Web Dev career enters ! I realized the amount of coding skills I had learned back in high school and in my free time were not enough to get my site where I wanted it. I needed to learn more programming languages. I want to spark joy and be successful on my own terms. This is why I chose to go from a blood banker who saves lives to a web developer who saves code. Dee FG
